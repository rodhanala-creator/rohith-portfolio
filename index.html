Selected work

Two case studies. Both about quietly removing friction.

Case 01 Healthcare · Operations

Clinic operations workflow overhaul for a busy dental practice.

A small dental practice was spending too much time on manual admin, inconsistent follow-ups, and scattered internal communication. The goal: simplify daily operations, reduce missed tasks, and create a smoother patient experience.

The problem

  • Too many manual admin steps.
  • Delayed responses to enquiries.
  • No clear intake or follow-up flow.
  • Staff stuck in repetitive coordination.

What I did

  • Mapped the existing patient workflow.
  • Identified bottlenecks and high-friction admin tasks.
  • Designed a single intake → booking → follow-up flow.
  • Created clear handoffs and task ownership for the team.

The system

  • One enquiry intake route across channels.
  • Automatic routing of common requests.
  • Standard response templates.
  • Booking and reminder flow.
  • Internal process for follow-ups and updates.

Outcomes

  • Faster response times to patient enquiries.
  • Reduced admin duplication.
  • Clearer task ownership across staff.
  • More consistent follow-up process.
  • Improved internal organisation and team confidence.

Selected project example. Tools: no-code workflow builders, form builders, email automation, calendar/booking tools.

Case 02 Service business · Lead generation

Lead capture and follow-up system for a small service business.

Enquiries were coming in across multiple channels, but the business was losing potential clients because there was no structured follow-up. I designed a simple lead system that captured enquiries, organised them, and improved response consistency.

The problem

  • Unstructured incoming leads.
  • Slow or inconsistent follow-up.
  • No central view of enquiries.
  • Lost opportunities from cold leads.

What I did

  • Analysed the lead journey from first contact to booking.
  • Designed a simpler conversion path.
  • Set up the message flow and follow-up structure.
  • Built a lightweight system the team could maintain.

The system

  • Landing page and enquiry form.
  • Lead capture into a central tracker.
  • Auto-acknowledgment messaging.
  • Follow-up reminders for warm and dormant leads.
  • Simple dashboard for visibility.

Outcomes

  • Better lead visibility across the team.
  • Faster first response.
  • Fewer missed enquiries.
  • More structured follow-up cadence.
  • A cleaner client acquisition process.

Selected project example. Tools: landing page builder, form tool, CRM/tracker, email automation, AI-assisted message drafting.
